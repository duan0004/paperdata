### Is it worth targeting PRL after packaging?

**Yes, with a realistic risk rating: medium risk after H4/H5/H6, still high if packaged as mere reproducibility.** The scientific core is coherent and now passes the internal Plan-D gate, but the PRL pitch must be sharpened. The title/abstract should not sell this as another NANOGrav reanalysis. It should sell the general result:

> For PTA model comparison, absolute log-Bayes factors for new-physics spectra are controlled at the sub-nat level by the matched template/density stack; local free-spectrum KDE reconstructions can preserve ranking while failing absolute evidence reproduction, and curved astrophysical SMBHB spectra remain competitive with the leading cosmological templates.

That is a PRL-plausible methodological physics message because it affects interpretation of current and future PTA evidence for cosmological sources. It remains risky because some editors may classify the density-provenance part as technical validation; H4 is the strongest defense against that classification.
